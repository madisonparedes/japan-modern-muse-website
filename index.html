About

モダンミューズについて

Modern Muse started as my space for romanticizing real life. Now, this version is styled for a Japanese audience.

I kept my usual focus on travel, lifestyle, and beauty, but shifted the way everything is presented. The color palette uses warm neutrals, sakura pink, and deep green inspired by Japanese aesthetics. The typography is quieter and more readable, and there’s more whitespace between sections so the page feels calm instead of crowded. I also added small Japanese phrases and a simple language toggle to show how the site could eventually support bilingual content for Japanese readers.

Museletter ニュースレター

A cozy newsletter with playlists, travel recs, and little journal prompts. Written in my usual Modern Muse voice, but with softer visuals for Japan. 季節のムードボードや旅のおすすめ、心をととのえるための一言メモを、日本の読者も楽しめるようなデザインでお届けします。

The form is simple and bilingual on purpose. Japanese users expect clean forms, clear labels, and gentle copy instead of loud sales language.
